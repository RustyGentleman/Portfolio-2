Retroweb 2000s (May 2025)
A whirlwind of a month-long group project developed as a gift for a VTuber friend of mine by myself and some of our closest friends. As the year's crowned head of our secret Royal Celebratory Committee (the RCC) and self-appointed workhorse, my initial suggestion for the year's project was a retro, 2000s style website, with a hub-like homepage leading to personalized pages for each of the RCC's acting participants. However, it quickly grew into an eclectic point-and-click game, with the goal of collecting a key from each page and unlocking an ending page full of gifs and birthday messages. It features a crafting system, a Clippy-like companion, and a media player with unlockable songs throughout the website, among others. Play Repository
